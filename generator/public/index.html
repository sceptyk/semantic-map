What is it?

This is a semantic map of Dublin based on Twitter activity. We use public tweets with coordinates to map the most popular spots, graph the popularity hours and present word-clouds for specific regions. If you click on the map you will change the base region and see from where people are coming to this place. You may filter results as well by keywords or by advanced options (open the menu by clicking on in top right corner.
